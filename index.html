Greetings!

I'm Andrew. Welcome to my site!

I’m a highly self-motivated Front End Developer with an eye for detail and a strict adherence to standards and clean code. I’ve been programming for five years, three of which have been spent developing for the web.

I harbor an obsession of all things web dev, from designing mockups in Sketch to writing middleware in Node.js. I have propensity to follow best practices and a drive to learn everything, though I recognize the importance of filtering out all the noise of tools and frameworks to get most familiar with the rudimentary building blocks of the web.

For me, web development is an outlet for both the creative and the logical person in me. I also find it a fascinating and exciting industry to be in. I don’t see development as work, but rather a craft to be fine-tuned over time.

Other than coding, some hobbies of mine include running, hiking, reading, and boardgames.
